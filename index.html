About Me

If you're wondering who I am...
I’m Farouq , a 23 years old Front-end developer. Nigerian currently living in Dublin, Ireland.


After my college degree in "Mass Communication" which explored Broadcasting communications, Public relation and Advertising, I decided to change career path to client-side programming due to my aptitude in digital technology. I started my masters programme in "Applied Digital Media" in February 2018, straight into the world of Javascript using p5*.

I found it hard at first because i had no programming experience at the time. I eventually fall in love with Javascript when i strated to understand it and realised how amazing it is (Loops, Variables, Arrays, GUI, Mapping, Network Data). And also thanks to a great tutor, Ruairi Murphy, who relentlessly made it fun for me.


At the completion of my masters, I was conversant with Web developement and Publishing, Game design, 3D modelling, Interaction Design and Data Visualization but my intrest in web development and UI/UX made me took the career path as a Front-end Developer.


I always want to learn more, do more and be more and i guess that was what made me learn Wordpress, SEO, PPC, SASS on my own and also took online courses.


I'm hard working, super curious, passionate, dedicated, and also a fast learner!


My current stack of languages/technologies is:

HTML5 - CSS3 - JAVASCRIPT - SASS - JQUERY - PHP - WordPress - Adobe Photoshop - InVision
